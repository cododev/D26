General Questions

DeediX Technologies is a full-service IT solutions provider specializing in cloud computing, cybersecurity, custom software development, IT consulting, and professional training. We help businesses leverage technology to achieve their goals through innovative and reliable solutions.

We were founded in 2022 and have over 5 years of collective experience in the IT industry. Our team brings expertise from working with various enterprises and technology platforms.

We serve a wide range of industries including healthcare, finance, education, retail, manufacturing, and professional services. Our solutions are tailored to meet the specific needs of each industry sector.

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups and small businesses to large enterprises. Our solutions are scalable and can be customized to fit your budget and requirements.
