Refund & Return Policy

Shop with confidence. We stand behind our products with a fair and transparent refund policy.

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

Our Commitment

At DeediX Shop, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We understand that sometimes products may not meet your expectations or may arrive damaged. This Refund & Return Policy outlines the conditions and procedures for returning products and receiving refunds.

Our Guarantee: We offer a 7-day return policy on most products and full refunds for defective or damaged items.

Return Time Frames

Standard Return Period

You may return most items within 7 days of receiving your order for a full refund or exchange, provided the items meet our return conditions.

Extended Return Period

The following items qualify for a 14-day return period:

  • Laptops and computers
  • Gaming consoles (unopened)
  • High-value items over ₦500,000
Important: The return period begins from the date you receive the item, not the purchase date. Items must be returned within the specified timeframe to qualify for a refund.

Eligible Items for Return

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

  • Unused and Unopened: Items must be in their original condition, unworn, unused, and in original packaging
  • All Accessories Included: All original accessories, manuals, and documentation must be included
  • Original Packaging: Items must be in original manufacturer packaging (if applicable)
  • Proof of Purchase: You must provide your order number or receipt
  • No Physical Damage: Items must not show signs of wear, damage, or modification

Items with Special Conditions

  • Smartphones & Tablets: Must have original IMEI intact, no screen damage, and all protective films in place
  • Earbuds & Headphones: Must be unopened due to hygiene reasons (unless defective)
  • Software & Digital Products: Non-returnable once activated or downloaded

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or refunded unless they are defective:

  • Earbuds, earphones, and in-ear headphones (opened packages)
  • Software, games, and digital download codes (once activated)
  • Gift cards and vouchers
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance"
  • Personalized or custom-ordered items
  • Items purchased during special promotions (check promotion terms)
  • Items damaged due to misuse or improper handling
Note: Even if an item is non-returnable, we will always honor our warranty obligations for defective products.

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, we will provide a full refund or replacement at no additional cost.

What to Do:

  1. Inspect Upon Delivery: Check your items immediately upon delivery
  2. Document the Issue: Take clear photos or videos of the defect or damage
  3. Contact Us Within 48 Hours: Report the issue via email, WhatsApp, or phone
  4. Provide Details: Include your order number, photos, and description of the issue
Fast Resolution: We will arrange for pickup and replacement within 2-3 business days for defective items.

How to Return an Item

Step-by-Step Return Process:

1
Initiate Return Request
Contact us via email (shop@deedixtech.com), WhatsApp (+234 807 438-7880), or through our contact page within the return period.
2
Provide Order Information
Share your order number, item details, and reason for return. Attach photos if applicable.
3
Receive Return Authorization
We will review your request and provide a Return Authorization (RA) number within 24 hours.
4
Package Your Item
Securely pack the item in its original packaging with all accessories. Include the RA number inside the package.
5
Ship or Arrange Pickup
For defective items: We arrange free pickup. For other returns: Ship to our address (provided with RA number).
6
Inspection & Refund
Once we receive the item, we will inspect it and process your refund within 3-5 business days.

Refund Methods & Timeline

How You Will Receive Your Refund

  • Card Payments: Refunded to the original payment method (3-7 business days)
  • Bank Transfer: Refunded to your bank account (2-3 business days)
  • Cash on Delivery: Refunded via bank transfer to your provided account

Refund Processing Time

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:

  • Inspection: 1-2 business days
  • Refund Initiation: 1 business day
  • Bank Processing: 2-7 business days (depending on your bank)
Total Timeframe: You should receive your refund within 5-10 business days from when we receive your return.

Return Shipping Costs

Who Pays for Return Shipping?

  • Defective or Damaged Items: We pay for return shipping and arrange pickup
  • Wrong Item Sent: We pay for return shipping and arrange pickup
  • Change of Mind: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • Non-Defective Returns: Customer pays for return shipping

Return Shipping Address: (Provided with your Return Authorization number)

Exchanges

We currently process exchanges as returns and new orders. To exchange an item:

  1. Return the original item following our return process
  2. Once the return is processed, place a new order for the desired item
  3. We will prioritize processing your new order
Priority Exchanges: For defective items, we can ship the replacement immediately upon receiving your return request.

Warranty Information

All products sold on DeediX Shop come with warranty protection:

  • New Products: Covered by manufacturer's warranty (6 months to 2 years depending on product)
  • Used Products: Covered by our 30-day warranty
  • Warranty Claims: Contact us with your order number and issue description

Warranty coverage is separate from our return policy and extends beyond the return period.
