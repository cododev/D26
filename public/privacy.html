Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

At DeediX Technologies, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our services. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Identity Information: Date of birth, national ID, or passport number.
  • Professional Information: Job title, company name, and industry.

b. Technical Information

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, and the pages you visit.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

c. Usage Data

  • Service Usage: Details about how you use our services, including the services you viewed or searched for, page response times, and download errors.

2. How We Use Your Information

a. To Provide and Improve Our Services

  • To deliver the services you request.
  • To improve and personalize your experience with our services.

b. Communication

  • To communicate with you about your account, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • To send you updates, newsletters, and marketing communications (you can opt-out at any time).

c. Legal and Regulatory Compliance

  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests or protect our rights.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

a. Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processors, data analysis, email delivery, hosting services, and customer service.

b. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

c. Legal Obligations

We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, court orders, or to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.

4. Security of Your Information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Despite our efforts, no security measure is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data.
  • Rectification: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your personal data to another service provider.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us here.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including for legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and indicating the date of the latest revision.

8. Acknowledgement

By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy policy and agree to our collection, use, and sharing of your information as described.
